What I got for christmas 2016

December 31, 2016December 3, 2017 Siana Jade

Hello again to anyone who kindly takes the time to read my blog, i am back for another post on the things i got for christmas.

before i get into the presents i would just like to put out a disclaimer to say that i am in no way bragging or intending to brag i only wish to review and recommend to anyone interested.

  2. as i am obsessed with foxes, i got a fair few fox themed stocking fillers including- knee high fox socks, fox slippers from fatface, a fox colouring book, a fox jumper from mantaray, and Â fox calendar for 2017.
