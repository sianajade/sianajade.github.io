Post-Christmas sales HAUL

January 7, 2018 Siana Jade

Hello and welcome to a very exciting post (for me at least). In case you didn’t know, my present from my parents for Christmas was money to spend on the post-Christmas sales. So I used it for exactly that and I can’t wait to show you what I bought.

 

WARNING: There will be descriptions of female underwear in this post so if that kind of thing offends you or you find it too explicit or awkward then you might want to leave now. If you want to read about the rest of the stuff I bought then i’ll let you know that the underwear buys will be at the bottom, found just after the end of the descriptions and photos of my things from Pull&Bear.

Also, this is once again not a post to be boastful, materialistic, or anything like that. I am doing it at the request of the people who voted on my Instagram poll. Anyway, that said, let’s talk about the more exciting things!..

 

Urban Outfitters

From Urban Outfitters I finally bought the oh so popular and sought after Mario Bedescu facial spray. It is so refreshing and moisturising and smells delicious!

H&M

I have been wanting a longer and thinner scarf than my usual thick ones for a while now and I finally found this gorgeous, soft, light grey scarf in H&M which turned out to be an absolute bargain!

Pandora

As you may know, I received a Pandora gift card from my Grandparents for Christmas so I payed a rather exciting visit to pandora and bought the rose gold princess tiara ring and I couldn’t be happier about it! The gift card payed for half of it so I used my own money to pay for the rest of it but boy was it worth it! I’ve wanted a princess ring for a loooong time (especially because it reminds me of certain personal memories and means a lot to me).

Pull&Bear

So from Pull&Bear during the first shopping trip, I bought a little black dress (LBD), I’ve been looking for one for ages and this velvet, cold shoulder, swing dress was just perfect! I’m afraid the photo doesn’t do it justice but trust me, it is very pretty.

I then bought a gorgeous, long, fashion coat made of a sort of grey wool fabric that is soooo soft and stylish. I love it! I’ve again, been looking for a long fashion coat for a long time. The photograph doesn’t exactly do this any justice either but you might find a picture of me wearing it on my Instagram sometime in the future.

While shopping the first time I was eyeing up a gorgeous jumper with colourful stripes on the front and so on the second shopping trip I bought one! It’s so comfy and gorgeous I just love it!

Victoria’s Secret

Underwear!!! I am a big fan of Victoria’s secret underwear as you might know because I got some of their pants for Christmas.

I bought three pairs of panties which are all the same kind of style with different patterns and are so cute!

I then bought a t-shirt bra with a checked green pattern, a ‘bondage’ style scalloped edge bralette in a baby blue colour, and a sports bra with bright pink and burgundy stripes.

 

I love everything that I bought and I am very thankful for my Christmas money. I also put a lot of my own money towards everything. I hope you enjoyed reading about my sales purchases and that maybe you went sales shopping yourself and got some great buys!

Happy sales shopping,

Siana xxx
